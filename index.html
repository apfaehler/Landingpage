Second Hand Success

Second Hand Success is an online marketplace for buying and selling gently used couches. Our mission is to provide an affordable and convenient option for those in the community who can't afford new couches, while also giving back to the community through charitable donations.
We accept couch donations from individuals looking to give back to their community, and a percentage of each sale will be donated to a charity.

Our Services

Want your couch out of sight out of mind? We can help with that. We offer pick up services of your couch so you can say goodbye forever and part ways. Don't worry, we will give it to a good home.

How will we find a couch a new home you ask? Oh you didnt? Well we will tell you anyway. We List them on our site as well as having a presence on Facebook marketplace if you prefere that platform better.

We keep our promise. Money we make off these couaches go back to helping the community. A percentage of our earnings goes back to help others.
